# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Educational Platform

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics educational platform - an interactive learning environment designed to teach the fundamentals and advanced concepts of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics.

## About

This platform provides comprehensive educational resources on:
- Introduction to Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics
- Fundamentals of Robotics
- AI Foundations for Robotics
- Sensors, Actuators, and Embodiment
- Control Systems and Feedback Theory
- Advanced Topics in Humanoid Robotics

## Features

- Interactive learning modules
- AI-powered tutoring and assessment
- Personalized learning paths
- Multilingual support (Urdu localization)
- Hands-on robotics simulations
- Expert mentorship through AI agents

## Getting Started

1. Sign up for an account
2. Complete the initial assessment
3. Follow your personalized learning path
4. Engage with AI tutors and mentors
5. Track your progress and achievements

## Tech Stack

- Frontend: Docusaurus (React-based)
- Backend: Node.js/TypeScript
- Authentication: Better-Auth
- AI Agents: Custom implementations
- Translation: Multilingual NLP models
